How Classes Work

Six things we do
differently in every class.

No boring drills. No memorised templates. Every class at Lumina is built around real English — the kind that works in the exam room and in real life.

01

Learn from Real Content

We use movies, talks, and real conversations — not old exam recordings. You start to hear how English actually sounds, spoken by real people.

02

Speak Every Single Class

You practise speaking from day one. No waiting, no shyness corner. You build confidence by using English — out loud, every session.

03

Read and Listen Smarter

Simple strategies to find answers fast — in the IELTS exam and in real university classes. Less panic, more speed, better results.

04

Build Real Vocabulary

Words from real conversations — not lists you memorise and forget. You pick up language the natural way: by hearing it used in context.

05

Write Step by Step

IELTS essays, emails, and academic writing — broken down clearly and practised every week. You write your own ideas in clean, correct English.

06

Full Exam Practice

Mock IELTS tests, honest feedback, and a clear picture of where you stand. You know exactly what to improve before exam day.

IELTS at Lumina

The exam is
just the start.

There are two ways to prepare for IELTS. One is tricks and shortcuts. The other is building real English — so the exam feels manageable, and everything after it feels possible.

"We train you for the exam and for the life that comes after it."
How we prepare you for IELTS
01

Real listening practice

We use real clips from documentaries, talks, and conversations — not old exam recordings. Your ears adjust to how English actually sounds.

02

Speaking that sounds natural

You practise speaking every class, so by exam day it feels normal — not scary. IELTS examiners notice the difference.

03

Reading without panic

Simple strategies to find answers quickly. You don't need to read every word — you need to know where to look.

04

Writing your own ideas

No templates. You learn to organise your thoughts and write clearly. That's what actually gets a good IELTS writing score.

05

Vocabulary you actually use

Words from real content — not memorised lists. You hear them, use them in class, and they stick.

Life After IELTS

Pass the exam.
Then actually be ready.

Your IELTS score gets you in. But then what? Lumina prepares you for the real moments you'll face — so you don't arrive abroad and freeze.

01

In the Classroom

Your professor opens the floor for questions. Do you stay quiet — or do you put your hand up? Lumina builds the confidence to speak, from your very first week.

02

In Group Projects

Working with classmates from different countries — discussing, planning, disagreeing. You will practise all of this at Lumina before you get there.

03

Writing to Your Professor

Emailing a professor or university office is its own skill. Lumina's writing practice covers exactly this — clear, polite, and professional.

04

In Job Applications

Part-time work, internship interviews, campus opportunities — you will be ready to present yourself in English clearly and confidently.

Lumina prepares you for the whole journey — the exam, the move, and the life that follows.
